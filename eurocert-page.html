Institute of Testing & Certification (ITC)

Institute of Testing and Certification (India) Pvt. Ltd.

We Don't Just Design for Safety. We Prove It.

Our partnership with the Institute of Testing and Certification (ITC) transforms your design's safety claims into irrefutable, accredited technical evidence.

The MDE-ITC Collaboration: The Foundation of Technical Truth

In essence, MDE designs the compliance into the machine, and ITC provides the accredited validation that the compliance was achieved. This partnership ensures that the "Excellence" in Machinery Design Excellence is not just an opinion, but a certified fact.

3 Ways This Collaboration Drives Your Success

We leverage the MDE-ITC synergy to ensure your journey from design to market approval is predictable, proven, and accelerated.

1

Accredited Validation: From Claim to Fact

This collaboration validates every engineering decision with hard, objective data.

  • MDE's Role (The Design): We design systems to comply with standards like IEC 60204-1.
  • ITC's Role (The Proof): As an NABL-accredited (ISO/IEC 17025) lab, ITC performs physical tests for third-party proof.
The MDE-ITC Advantage: You move from "we think it's safe" to "here is the accredited lab report that proves it."
2

Streamlined Certification & Risk Reduction

We de-risk the entire certification process, making market entry predictable.

  • MDE's Role (Audit Readiness): We prepare documentation and ensure the prototype is "audit-ready."
  • ITC's Role (Gap Identification): They conduct pre-certification testing (e.g., IP Code validation) to resolve compliance gaps before engaging the Notified Body.
The MDE-ITC Advantage: This prevents costly redesigns and delays, creating a predictable, streamlined path to certification.
3

Bulletproof Technical File & Final Approval

We establish an unbreakable chain of compliance evidence.

  • How it Works (The Chain): ITC's technical reports are the foundational evidence required by Notified Bodies like Eurocert.
  • MDE's Role (Optimization): Our process ensures your design is optimized to pass these tests seamlessly.
The MDE-ITC Advantage: We build a bullet-proof technical file that accelerates final approval and gives you unwavering confidence in your product's safety and quality.
*ITC is the Institute of Testing and Certification (India) Pvt. Ltd.*

Eurocert - Multinational Notified Body

Eurocert - Multinational Notified Body

Your Gateway to Global Market Access

As a Notified Body (NoBo 2293), Eurocert provides the CE marking and certification services you need to legally sell your machinery in European and global markets.

The MDE-Eurocert Partnership: Design Meets Certification

MDE ensures your machine is designed for compliance, while Eurocert provides the official certification that opens doors to international markets. Together, we transform your innovation into market-ready products.

3 Ways Eurocert Partnership Accelerates Your Market Entry

We leverage the MDE-Eurocert synergy to ensure your path from design to CE marking is streamlined, compliant, and accelerated.

1

CE Marking & Market Access

Your direct route to European and international markets.

  • MDE's Role (Compliance Design): We design machinery to meet Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC and harmonized standards.
  • Eurocert's Role (Official Certification): As a Notified Body, they issue CE certificates and technical file approval for market access.
The MDE-Eurocert Advantage: From concept to CE mark - we handle both design compliance and certification in one seamless process.
2

Technical File Excellence

Build a bulletproof technical documentation package.

  • MDE's Role (Documentation): We prepare comprehensive technical files including risk assessments, drawings, and compliance declarations.
  • Eurocert's Role (Validation): They review and validate your technical file against EU requirements for CE marking approval.
The MDE-Eurocert Advantage: No back-and-forth delays - our documentation is pre-validated to Eurocert's standards.
3

Global Compliance Confidence

Expand beyond Europe with international recognition.

  • MDE's Role (Multi-Market Design): We design for compliance with international standards (ISO, IEC) that work globally.
  • Eurocert's Role (Global Recognition): Their certifications are recognized worldwide, opening doors to markets beyond Europe.
The MDE-Eurocert Advantage: One certification process, global market access - from EU to Asia, Americas, and beyond.
*Eurocert is a Multinational Notified Body (NoBo 2293) for Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC*
