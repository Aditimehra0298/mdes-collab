What is MDES?

MDES (Machinery Design Excellence) is India's premier platform for machinery innovators, engineers, and solution providers to showcase cutting-edge technologies, validate ideas, and grow business opportunities.

Whether you're a machinery designer, component supplier, software provider, or R&D lab - if you're ready to future-proof your business, this platform is for you.

Reimagine Automation & Manufacturing Technology

ENGIMACH has been a major contributor to the growth of the engineering machinery and machine tools sector for over 25 years, with 15 exhibitions, participation from over 35 countries, over 6850 exhibitors, and almost a million visitors. It is well-known for the high calibre of its exhibitors and visitors.

Who can participate?

+

Can international companies join?

+

Yes, MDES is designed for global participation.

What does Damnart deliver?

+

Is there a sponsorship or collaboration cost?

+

We offers flexible collaboration packages tailored to your needs, ranging from digital showcasing to full-featured Wall of Excellence participation.

Who should I contact for partnership inquiries?

+

How do meetings work?

+

How do I get leads?

+

All consolidated leads are shared post-event.

Collaborators gain access to a high-traffic pavilion visited by over a million industry stakeholders, with branding, digital showcasing, and networking opportunities, all backed by our Excellence Partner ITC and principal sponsor EUROCERT.

If my organization isn't exhibiting at ENGIMACH, can we still collaborate?

+

Yes, you can join MDE and gain significant visibility, brand presence, and networking opportunities at ENGIMACH.

Is on-site presence required for collaboration?

+

Not at all. You can collaborate and participate virtually from anywhere in the world.
