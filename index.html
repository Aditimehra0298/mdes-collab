What is MDES?

MDES (Machinery Design Excellence) is India's premier platform for machinery innovators, engineers, and solution providers to showcase cutting-edge technologies, validate ideas, and grow business opportunities.

Whether you're a machinery designer, component supplier, software provider, or R&D lab - if you're ready to future-proof your business, this platform is for you.

About DamnArt

Company Overview

DAMNART—a design and technology company founded in Dubai with a presence in India, the UK, Canada, and the US—specializes in AI-driven conformity assessment training. Our expertise aligns seamlessly with the global conformity assessment system's objectives, strategic initiatives, and its mission to foster competence and trust worldwide.

DAMNART collaborates with EUROCERT to put up a Pavilion of Machinery Design Excellence at ENGIMACH 2025, giving an opportunity to Machinery Manufacturers to showcase their enterprise, design and products to millions of visitors from across the globe. Our mission is to empower machinery industries with stronger visibility and recognition, and more partner leads in today's global competition.
